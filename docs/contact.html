The patient portal is a convenient, secure tool providing 24-hour access. The site allows you to communicate directly with your physician’s office and access your medical history. The portal ensures you receive reminders and notifications from your doctor’s office. In addition, you can view office visit notes, billing statements, exchange private messages with your doctor’s office, request prescription refills and request new appointments.
