Fees and Payment Information

Our fees are comparable to those charged for similar services in our community. Payment at the time of service is required to help contain our costs and keep our fees reasonable. Co-payments and deductibles are due at the time of service. For your convenience, we do accept both Visa and MasterCard.

Please provide accurate insurance records including your carrier and policy number. You may wish to speak with our billing department to verify that we participate with your particular insurance plan. We are contracted with most insurance plans. Please be aware that if your insurance has not paid your charges within sixty days, these charges will become your responsibility. Please let us know of any changes in your insurance as soon as they occur.

Emergencies

If a medical condition arises when our office is closed and you do not feel it will wait until the office re-opens, please call 911. A provider can be reached by calling our answering service at 770-928-5206. Please note that a nominal fee will be charged for this service.
